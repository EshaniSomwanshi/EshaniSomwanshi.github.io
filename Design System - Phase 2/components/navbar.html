Accessibility Considerations

Ensuring clear aria-labels to help screen-readers identify the navigation section. Additionally include attributes like aria-controls and aria-expanded on the toggle button to communicate its behavior to assistive technologies. Structure nav links with clear, descriptive text and maintain a logical tab order for smooth keyboard navigation.
