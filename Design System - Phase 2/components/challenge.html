Accessibility Considerations

Assigned roles and aria labels to define meaningful content areas and provide clear labels for assistive technologies. Maintaining high color contrast between text and background images ensures support for readability. Use of consistent layout and text structure across desktop and mobile views to ensure a predictable experience for all users.
